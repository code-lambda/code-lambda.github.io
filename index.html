In what projects are we a good fit?

We've worked with all sorts of project requirements across different domains. But we've specifically gathered expertise for these three:

  • 01 Existing businesses looking to modernize/digitalize their processes.

    Businesses tend to evolve from very simple processes into labor-intensive manual systems. Think of complex spreadsheets and large data-entry workflows. Modernizing these systems allows your staff to focus on things that actually make you money.

  • 02 Early startups considering building out a web app instead of mobile to save costs

    Mobile apps are all the rage, and everyone wants one. What most don't know is that building a web application is a more economical approach for most cases. We've seen lots of promising ideas unnecessarily burn capital by building a mobile app when a web app would've done the job. We build these web apps with flexible backends so it can be used by a mobile app in the future.

  • 03 Entrepreneurs looking to validate their ideas using a prototype.

    A very unfortunate reality is that jumping into development without prototyping your idea will lead to longer time to your first customer. We can build out low-fidelity prototypes using Figma, so you can identify the gaps in your idea before committing to a team of developers.
